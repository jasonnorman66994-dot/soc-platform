# Pricing Strategy

## Principles
- Price on risk reduction and response speed, not raw log volume.
- Preserve a low-friction entry while gating high-value automation.
- Align enterprise pricing with compliance and integration depth.

## Tiers

### Free
- Target: technical evaluators
- Includes: core incident views, limited event retention, basic detections
- Excludes: advanced detection marketplace packs and automated response workflows

### Pro ($2,500/month baseline)
- Target: scaling teams needing daily SOC operations
- Includes: advanced detections, response actions, executive dashboards, API-key ingestion
- Limits: moderate event throughput and user seats

### Enterprise (Custom)
- Target: regulated and larger organizations
- Includes: custom integrations, private deployment options, compliance export pipelines, premium support
- Commercials: annual contract, onboarding fee, optional implementation services

## Packaging Levers
- Detection pack subscriptions by vertical
- Extra response automation connectors
- Data retention extensions
- Additional analyst seats

## Land-and-Expand Motion
1. Start with Free and one design-team workflow.
2. Move to Pro when action automation and dashboard accountability are required.
3. Upgrade to Enterprise for compliance and scale.
