# Competitive Positioning

## Positioning Statement
Nexus SOC is the fastest path from security signal to coordinated action for lean and scaling security teams.

## Against Legacy SIEM Suites
- Legacy: powerful but complex onboarding and long time-to-value.
- Nexus: opinionated setup, immediate simulation-driven proof, integrated SOAR workflows.

## Against Point SOAR Tools
- Point SOAR requires separate SIEM context and correlation stacks.
- Nexus combines ingestion, detection, correlation, response, and metrics in one platform.

## Against Generic Log Analytics
- Generic analytics lacks security-specific response orchestration and role-based incident workflow.
- Nexus is purpose-built for SOC operations and executive reporting.

## Sentinel/Splunk Comparison Narrative
- Splunk/Sentinel excel in broad ecosystem maturity and deep enterprise footprint.
- Nexus wins where buyers need rapid deployment, integrated response, and founder-level support with lower operational overhead.

## Core Differentiators
- Multi-tenant isolation as a first-class primitive
- Built-in detection marketplace packs
- Realistic attack simulation workflow for buyer confidence
- Executive metrics and AI analyst in one UX
