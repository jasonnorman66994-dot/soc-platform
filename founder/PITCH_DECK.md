# Nexus SOC Pitch Deck (Investor-Ready)

## 1. Problem
Security teams are drowning in fragmented tools, noisy alerts, and slow incident response. Mid-market organizations cannot afford a full enterprise SOC stack and do not have enough analysts to close the gap.

## 2. Solution
Nexus SOC is a multi-tenant SIEM + SOAR + AI analyst platform that turns detection into action fast:
- Tenant-isolated event pipeline
- Detection marketplace rule packs
- Built-in response orchestration
- Executive-level risk and response metrics

## 3. Product Proof
Live product demonstrates:
- API-key ingestion
- Realistic attack simulation chain
- Correlated incidents
- AI-guided next steps
- Audit trail for SOC2-style controls

## 4. Why Now
- AI-assisted attacks are increasing alert volume and sophistication.
- Buyer demand is shifting toward integrated platforms that reduce tool sprawl.
- Security leaders need measurable metrics (MTTD/MTTR) for board reporting.

## 5. Market
- Initial ICP: 200-5000 employee companies with lean security teams
- Vertical wedge: SaaS, fintech, healthcare-tech
- Expansion: MSSPs and channel partners

## 6. Business Model
- Subscription SaaS with tiered pricing
- Platform upsell via premium detection packs and workflow integrations
- Enterprise add-ons for compliance reporting and private deployment

## 7. Competition
Competes with legacy SIEM tooling by prioritizing speed-to-value and integrated response over heavy deployment complexity.

## 8. Go-To-Market
- Founder-led sales to design partners
- Incident simulation demo as acquisition engine
- Content + webinars around measurable SOC outcomes

## 9. Metrics That Matter
- Trial-to-paid conversion
- Median time to first detection
- Median time to first response action
- Expansion revenue from Free -> Pro -> Enterprise

## 10. Ask
Seeking capital and strategic introductions to accelerate:
- Enterprise integrations
- Sales engineering
- Compliance automation
